# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## What is a Cookie?

A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function or function more efficiently, and to provide information to website owners.

## How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

### Necessary Cookies (Always Enabled)

These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of the website and cannot be disabled in our systems.

- **Authentication**: Maintains your login state

- **Security**: Prevents cross-site request forgery attacks

- **Session Management**: Tracks your browsing session

- **Load Balancing**: Distributes traffic among servers

### Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance website functionality and personalization.

- **Language Preference:** Remember your chosen language

- **Theme Settings:** Dark/Light Mode Preference

- **Interface Layout:** Save your interface customization settings

- **Recently Used:** Remember your recently used features

### Cookie Analysis

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the website.

- **Google Analytics**: Website usage statistics

- **Performance Monitoring**: Page load time and error tracking

- **User Behavior**: Feature usage analysis

- **A/B Testing**: Testing the user experience of different versions

### Advertising Cookies (where applicable)

We currently do not use advertising cookies, but may add them in the future:

- **Ad Targeting**: Interest-based advertising

- **Conversion Tracking**: Measuring ad performance

- **Redirection**: Showing relevant ads to previous visitors

## Managing your cookie preferences

### Browser settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

**Chrome:**

1. Click menu > Settings > Advanced > Privacy and security > Cookies Other Website Data

2. Choose Your Preferences

**Firefox:**

1. Click Menu > Options > Privacy & Security

2. Adjust settings in the Cookies and Website Data section

**Safari:**

1. Safari > Preferences > Privacy

2. Manage Cookies and Website Data

**Edge:**

1. Menu > Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

2. Cookies and Stored Data

### Our Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you will see a Cookie banner that allows you to:

- Accept all cookies

- Accept only necessary cookies

- Customize your preferences

- Learn more

### Cookie Preference Center

You can manage your cookie settings at any time through our Preference Center:

- View all cookie categories

- Enable or disable specific types of cookies

- View detailed cookie information

- Save your preferences

## The Impact of Cookies on Functionality

### If you disable cookies

- **Necessary Cookies**: The website may not function properly

- **Functionality Cookies**: You may need to reset your preferences.

- **Analysis Cookies**: They won't affect your experience, but we can't improve the service.

- **Advertising Cookies**: You won't see personalized ads.

### Mobile Devices

On mobile devices, cookies work similarly, but management may differ slightly:

- **iOS Safari**: Settings > Safari > Block all cookies

- **Android Chrome**: Chrome app > Settings > Site settings > Cookies

## Cookie Security

We take steps to ensure the security of cookies:

- **Security Flag**: Transmitted only over HTTPS

- **HttpOnly Flag**: Prevents JavaScript from accessing sensitive cookies

- **SameSite Attribute**: Prevents cross-site request forgery

- **Regular Cleanup**: Automatically deletes expired cookies
