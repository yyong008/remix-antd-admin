# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## 1. Information Collection

### 1.1 Types of Information We Collect

- **Account Information**: Email address, username, password (encrypted storage)

- **Personal Profile**: Avatar, display name, personal preferences

- **Usage Data**: Feature usage, interaction logs, error logs

- **Technical Information**: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

- Cookies and similar technologies

- Website analytics data

- Performance and diagnostic data

## 2. Information Use

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain our services

- Process transactions and send related notifications

- Improve and personalize the user experience

- Provide customer support

- Detect, prevent, and resolve technical issues

- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. Information Sharing

We will not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

- With your explicit consent

- With service providers (under strict confidentiality agreements)

- To comply with legal requirements or court orders

- To protect our rights and security

## 4. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)

- Password hash storage

- Regular security audits

- Access control

- Data backup and recovery

## 5. Data Retention

- Account data: During account activity and 30 days after deletion

- Usage data: Retained for up to 2 years

- Payment information: Retained as required by law

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access and update your personal information

- Delete your account and data

- Export your data

- Restrict or object to certain data processing

- Withdraw consent

## 7. Cookie Policy

We use cookies to:

- Maintain login status

- Remember user preferences

- Analyze website usage

- Personalized Experience

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
